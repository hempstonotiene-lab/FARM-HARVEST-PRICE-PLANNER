How the Farm Harvest & Price Planner works

1. Farmer input

Capture crop, planting date, location, and profile details through a simple mobile-first form.

2. Prediction engine

Blend crop rules, county climate patterns, live weather summaries, and market datasets on the backend.

3. Decision insight
